Donations


Things

Donations of gently-used books, recent magazines, CDs, DVDs and Blu-rays are greatly appreciated and are tax-deductible. You can make donations at both libraries.

Time

Donate your time and talents by volunteering with the Friends. Join a great group of people who are enthusiastic about books, libraries, reading and literacy.

Money

Monetary donations are tax-deductible. If you'd like to make your donation in honor of, or in memory of, a person or event, you can use this form.

You can send your check and form to the address on the form or you can give the form to a volunteer at the Loma Colorado Friends' Store and pay by cash, check, or credit card.

About Us


Friends of the Library of Rio Ranch, Inc. is an all-volunteer, membership organization. We were incorporated in 1982 as a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit corporation.

We fund all programs and activities for children, teens and adults at the Loma Colorado Library and Esther Bone Memorial Library in Rio Rancho, New Mexico. The biggest program we support each year is the summer-reading program.

We also fund the Books For Babies early literacy program.

Funding

Most of the money for library programs comes from books donated by the community and sold at our monthly book sales or at the Friends' stores in both libraries.

We also work with the library staff to apply for grants to support our activities.
