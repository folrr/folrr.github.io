Donate


Donations of gently-used books, recent magazines, CDs, DVDs and Blu-rays are greatly appreciated and are tax-deductible.

You can donate at both libraries.

*** Where to make donations ***

Donations are:

  • Added to the Libraries' collections
  • Sold in the two Friends' book stores
  • Sold at the monthly book sale
  • Selected books are sold online

Tax receipts are available for your donations at the Friends' Store at the Loma Colorado Library.

Volunteer


With no paid staff, Friends of the Library only exists because of the dedicated efforts of our volunteers. Donate your time and talents and join a great group of people who are enthusiastic about books, libraries, reading, and literacy.

There are a wide variety of volunteer opportunities: (click the title to see more information).

  • Every Thursday morning from 8 AM to about 11 AM in the Loma Colorado Library auditorium, we sort and price the books, magazines, CDs and DVDs that were donated during the previous week.

    If you would like to get involved, come to the auditorium any Thursday morning when the Library opens at 10 AM.

    Email the price-and-sort coordinator for more information.

  • The afternoon of the day before each monthly book sale, we meet at 3 PM to move books into the auditorium of the Loma Colorado Library and arrange them.

    On the Saturday after the sale closes, we meet at 2 PM to put the books away.

    During the sale, volunteers help buyers with their purchases, re-arrange books, and move more books into the sale.

    If you can arrange books or put books in boxes or move boxes of books, please join us.

  • Volunteers staff the Friends' store in the Loma Colorado Library from 10 AM to 6 PM Monday through Thursday and from 10 AM to 5 PM on Friday and Saturday.

    Most shifts are two hours long. Your shift can be weekly or the first and third weeks of the month or the second and fourth weeks.

    Email the Friends' Store coordinator for more information.

  • Each week volunteers re-stock and re-arrange books at the Pat McGuire Memorial FriendShop in the Esther Bone Memorial Library.

    Email the FriendsShop coordinator for more information.

  • In addition to the monthly book sales, Friends sell selected, high-value books on Amazon.

    Email the on-line coordinator for more information.

Give


Donations made to the Friends of the Library, a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt nonprofit corporation, are tax-deductible.

If you would like to make your donation in honor of, or in memory of, a person or event, you can use this form. Pre-printed forms are available at the Loma Colorado Friends' Store.

You can send your form and check to

Friends of the Library of Rio Rancho, Inc.
P.O. Box 15143
Rio Rancho, NM 87174

You can also drop your form and pay by cash, card or check at the Loma Colorado Friends' Store.

All tribute and memorial donations are promptly acknowledged.
