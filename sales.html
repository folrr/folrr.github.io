Book Sale Dates


Sales are the second Saturday of each month. Several times each year, two-day sales are held that include the Friday before the second Saturday.

The schedule for 2019:

  • February 8 & 9
  • March 9
  • April 13
  • May 10 & 11
  • June 8
  • July 12 & 13
  • August 10
  • September 13 & 14
  • October 12
  • November 9
  • December 14

Sales are in the auditorium of the Loma Colorado Library, 755 Loma Colorado Blvd. NE, Rio Rancho, NM.

Sales open at 10 AM, when the Library opens, and close at 2 PM.

Parking and admission are free.

You can sign-up to receive email notification before each sale.

Book Sale Prices


Thousands of gently-used books, and more, are available at the monthly book sales; many are like new. Come early for the best selection.

Book Sale Price List
Hardcover Books $2.00
Paperback Books $1.00
Coffee-table Books $4.00
DVDs & Blu-Ray From $2.00
CDs From $1.00
Friends Tote Bags $3.00
   Prices may change without notice.

In addition to the main sales area, individually-priced collectible books are often available on a table at the lobby entrance to the sale.

Children and young-reader books are available in the Loma Colorado Friends' stores.

Email Sign-up


To subscribe to the monthly book-sale notification email, fill in the three blanks and click Subscribe.

Shortly after clicking Subscribe, you will receive an email from Friends of the Library sent by MailChimp asking you to confirm your subscription. Please follow the instructions in that email to complete your subscription.

We do not sell, rent or loan anyone's name or email address. They are only used for Friends of the Library notifications.
