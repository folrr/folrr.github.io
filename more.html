Board of Directors


During the annual membership meeting in April, Friends elect members to the Board of Directors.

  • A directorâ€™s term is three years.
  • One-third of the board terms expire each year.
  • Elected directors may serve unlimited terms.
  • The board size has no maximum limit.
  • Each director is expected to participate in monthly book sales, luncheons, retreats, and every board meeting.
  • The Board elects Officers during the first board meeting following the annual membership meeting.

Unless in executive session, board meetings are open to the public. Meetings are usually the third Thursday of each month from 6 PM to 8 PM in the Loma Colorado Library lobby conference room. There is no meeting in December.
