Friends' Store


On the right as you enter the Loma Colorado Library, Friends' volunteers staff a full-service store.

The Friends' Store is open from 10 AM to 6 PM on Monday through Thursday and from 10 AM to 5 PM on Friday and Saturday when the library is open.

The store carries

  • Gently-used books
  • Music CDs
  • DVD and Blu-ray movies
  • Recent magazines
  • Jigsaw puzzles
  • Friends Tote bags
  • Hand-made Cards
  • Earbuds for computer use

Friends members receive an additional 10% discount off already low Friends’ Store prices by presenting their current Friends membership card at checkout.

Most charge cards are accepted with your purchase of $5 or more.

Your Friends membership application and dues payment are gladly accepted.

If you would like to volunteer, you can leave your contact information.

FriendShop


At the Esther Bone Memorial Library, the Pat McGuire Memorial FriendShop is a self-service store.

The FriendShop is available from noon to 8 PM on Tuesdays and from 9 AM to 5 PM on Wednesday through Saturday when the library is open.

Gently-used fiction and non-fiction books are arranged alphabetically by the author’s last name.

Please place your payment in the door slot on the left as you enter the shop.

Friends Tote bags and Earbuds for use with the public computers or any device with a 3.5 mm stereo audio jack are available at the Library's front desk.
